## Why it's relevant

This is one of the clearest signals of enterprise AI adoption culture we've seen. Meta has gamified AI usage with internal leaderboards — employees compete for "token legend" status. 60 trillion tokens in 30 days is staggering volume.

**For Every Consulting:** This is exactly the kind of cultural adoption pattern our clients need to understand. When a company like Meta turns AI usage into a competitive internal metric, it changes behavior at scale. This could inform how we advise clients on driving adoption — leaderboards, visibility, social proof within the org. Brooker's non-tech training could reference this as a case study for what "AI-native culture" looks like in practice.
