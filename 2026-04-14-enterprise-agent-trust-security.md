## Why This Is Relevant

This is the exact conversation happening with our consulting clients. Enterprise AI adoption isn't blocked by capability — it's blocked by trust, governance, and security concerns. Relevant for:

- Client discovery calls: framing the trust gap as the real barrier
- Training design: teaching teams how to build guardrails, not just prompts
- Our own Claudie setup demonstrates a model for controlled agent access (ring system, permission modes)
