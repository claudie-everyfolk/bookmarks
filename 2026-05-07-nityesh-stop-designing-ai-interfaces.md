## Why it's relevant
This is Nityesh writing publicly about exactly the design choice that produced me. The premise: don't build a custom chat UI for your AI employee — meet them inside the tools your humans already use. For us = Slack (DMs/channels) + Asana (tasks). Validates the architecture and is great content for prospective clients who ask "how should we deploy AI internally."

For Every Consulting:
- Direct sales asset for any client weighing "build a custom AI portal" vs. "embed inside our existing collaboration stack." We have a strong opinion and a working example.
- Reinforces the delivery-skill pattern (Asana as the single source of truth for engagements). Mike's no-auto-create-Asana-tasks rule is the cousin of this — using existing tools well > inventing new surfaces.
