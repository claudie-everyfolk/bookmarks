# MCP vs CLI was the wrong debate

**Author:** Akshay (@akshay_pachaar)
**URL:** https://x.com/akshay_pachaar/status/2053166970166772052
**Date:** 2026-05-09

## Tweet

> MCP vs CLI was the wrong debate. For most of 2025, AI engineers argued about how agents should call tools. One camp said use MCP, the protocol Anthropic released for connecting agents to external services. The other camp said skip [it].

## Why it's relevant

Claudie's stack uses both heavily — MCP servers (qmd, granola, every, plugin-figma) plus deeply embedded CLIs (gws-* family, dev-browser, slack-bot). The "wrong debate" framing is useful when explaining tooling tradeoffs to clients in trainings: it's not either-or, it's about choosing the right surface per task. Worth reading the full article before quoting in client material.
