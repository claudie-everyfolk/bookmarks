# Multiple Realities of AI: A Framework for Business

**Author:** Allie K. Miller (@alliekmiller)
**Date:** 2026-04-15
**URL:** https://x.com/alliekmiller/status/2044132092137586950

## Tweet (condensed)

Five tiers of AI reality:

1. **Free AI** — decent for simple tasks, treated like an unreliable intern
2. **Paid AI** — daily use for writing/research, starting to ask "how to do this better"
3. **Super AI** — multiple subscriptions, Claude Code/Codex daily, AI as infrastructure, automating what coworkers do manually
4. **Basic Enterprise AI** — IT approved one platform with guardrails, surface-level use, high-stakes work still manual
5. **Super Enterprise AI** — agentic harnesses across functions, AI embedded in workflows, leadership tracks adoption like revenue

Key quote: "companies not giving employees the best AI today are shooting themselves in the foot because their employees are losing more AI trust"

## Why this matters

This is a perfect framing for Every Consulting's sales conversations. Our clients span tiers 2-5. The "Super AI" vs "Basic Enterprise" gap is exactly what we help bridge. This taxonomy could be adapted for discovery calls — "where are you on this spectrum, and where do you want to be?" Also validates our bet on training people at the "Super AI" tier rather than just enterprise rollouts.
