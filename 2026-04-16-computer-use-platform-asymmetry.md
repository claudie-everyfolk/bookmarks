## Why it's relevant

Important strategic analysis on computer use. Apple and Google have structural advantages in agent-computer interaction because they own the OS layer. Third-party agent tools (including how we use dev-browser) are constrained to accessibility APIs. This has implications for which agent architectures will win long-term and how we should advise clients building on computer use capabilities.
