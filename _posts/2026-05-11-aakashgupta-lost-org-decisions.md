# Functionally-lost decisions

**Author:** Aakash Gupta (@aakashgupta)
**URL:** https://x.com/aakashgupta/status/2053867083285557437

## Tweet
> Try this on your team Monday. Ask any teammate why your team chose its current pricing model. The answer will either be "I wasn't here for that" or "let me dig up the deck." Both mean the decision is functionally lost.

This is a perfect opening line for an Every Consulting pitch about AI-augmented institutional memory. The "Why behind the decision is gone" framing connects directly to discovery-call work and process maps.
