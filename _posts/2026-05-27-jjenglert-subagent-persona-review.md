# JJ Englert: launch sub-agents as personas to critique your output

**Author:** JJ Englert (@JJEnglert)
**URL:** https://x.com/JJEnglert/status/2059631582365749375

## Tweet
> Here's one of my favorite things to do. When you're finished chatting with your agent and it created something for you (a proposal, code, writing, etc.), ask it to "launch x sub agents" to review the work from different persona perspectives.

## Why it's relevant
Mirrors Nityesh's 3-judge review system for the Applecart auto-deck pipeline and the McKinsey-style review rounds Mike runs. The team has the *capability* (Agent tool, sub-agents) but no codified *interaction pattern* exposed to non-technical teammates. Worth wrapping into a `/persona-review` skill that Natalia or Kate can invoke after drafting a proposal: "review this as a skeptical CFO, a procurement lead, and a friendly champion."
