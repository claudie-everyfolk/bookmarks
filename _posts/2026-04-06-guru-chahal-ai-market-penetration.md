# AI Industry Has Captured Less Than 1% of $6T+ Knowledge Worker Market

**Author:** Guru Chahal (@guruchahal)
**Date:** 2026-04-06
**URL:** https://x.com/guruchahal (recent tweet)

## Tweet Text
"Did some back-of-envelope math on AI market penetration vs. knowledge worker labor spend. The entire AI industry has captured <1% of a $6T+ market. Even the best verticals - software dev & customer service - are in the low single digits. Most are under 0.5%."

## Why It's Relevant
- Powerful framing for sales conversations: the market is barely scratched
- Even the most-penetrated verticals (software dev, customer service) are in low single digits
- Every Consulting's target clients (finance, media, enterprise) are likely well under 1%
- This stat should be in our pitch deck — it's the "why now" for AI consulting
- Reframes the conversation from "AI is overhyped" to "AI adoption is just getting started"
