## Why it's relevant

Anthropic's own guide on how Claude Code performs in large, real-world codebases — context management, where to start, what works and what breaks at scale. This is direct training material for Mike Taylor's Tech vertical: most of Every Consulting's engineering clients (Bloomberg, finance/PE engineering teams) work in large existing codebases, not greenfield projects. The "where to start" framing answers the most common objection from senior engineers — "Claude Code is fine for toy projects but useless in our monorepo." Worth pulling into Mike's curriculum as a reference and a counter-objection. Also a useful pattern source for how Claudie navigates her own growing skill/project repo.
