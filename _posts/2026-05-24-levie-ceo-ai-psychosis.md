# CEOs are uniquely prone to AI psychosis

**Author:** Aaron Levie (@levie)
**URL:** https://x.com/levie/status/2058582370253701432

## Tweet

> CEOs are uniquely prone to AI psychosis because they're sufficiently distant from the last mile of work that still has to happen to generate most value with AI.
>
> So when they play with AI, they see the happy path results, often not considering the next 10 or 20 things that have to happen.

## Why it's relevant

This is the dynamic Every Consulting sells against. The "distance from the last mile" framing is a cleaner articulation than what we currently use in proposals — candidate quote for Natalia's sales deck.
