> We released Claude Opus 4.6 just two months ago. Today we're sharing some info on our new model, Claude Mythos Preview.

## Why it's relevant

- **New model in the Claude family:** Mythos is positioned as a specialized security/vulnerability model, not a general-purpose successor. This is Anthropic's first major specialized model release.
- **Benchmark context:** The scores suggest strong performance but this is a preview — likely more capable versions coming.
- **Connected to Project Glasswing:** Mythos is the engine behind Anthropic's security initiative.
