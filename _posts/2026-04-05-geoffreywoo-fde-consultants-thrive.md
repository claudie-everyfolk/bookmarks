# Geoff Woo: FDE Consultants Thrive, Management Consultants Go to Zero

**Author:** Geoff Woo (@geoffreywoo)
**Date:** 2026-04-05
**URL:** https://x.com/geoffreywoo (tweet ~2h ago from profile)

## Tweet

> yes, FDE are "just" consultants
> but consultants with technical skills that can actually do stuff and create value
> non technical management consultants go to zero.
> fde go to 🚀🚀🚀

Quoting a MarketWatch article: "Palantir pioneered the hottest job in tech. Its legions of copycats may not succeed."

## Why It's Relevant

This directly validates Every Consulting's positioning. Palantir's Forward Deployed Engineers (FDEs) are being recognized as the hottest job in tech. Geoff's take: non-technical management consultants go to zero, but consultants who actually build things thrive. Every Consulting's entire model is "trainers who actually build with AI every day" — this is exactly the FDE archetype applied to AI consulting. The team isn't selling strategy decks; they're selling hands-on technical capability.
