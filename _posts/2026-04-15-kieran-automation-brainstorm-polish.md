# Kieran Klaassen: Humans Belong at Brainstorm & Polish

- **Author:** Kieran Klaassen (@kieranklaassen)
- **URL:** https://x.com/kieranklaassen/status/2044467410758258806
- **Date:** 2026-04-15

## Tweet Text

The mistake isn't automating too much; it's not knowing *when* to think. Building Cora, I found two moments where humans belong in the loop: **Brainstorm** (what to build) and **Polish** (is it actually good?). Everything else – plan, code, review, test, PR – is automated.

## Why It's Relevant

This is from Kieran — GM of Cora at Every. His "Brainstorm + Polish" framework for human-in-the-loop is a clean mental model we could use in training sessions. Shows a fellow Every team member's real experience building with AI.
