Alex Lieberman shared five direct exec quotes from Fortune 2000 firms:

- **CISO:** "There is an ocean-sized gap between hype and reality, which makes discerning what's real exhausting."
- **VP of AI engineering:** "Everyone acts like they're an expert, yet the main reason so few AI use cases have reached production in enterprises is because true expertise requires experience in scaled systems, enterprise politics, AI fluency, governance and guardrails, and deep process knowledge. Almost no one is actually an expert."
- **CTO:** "Our remit is to cut costs, but you can't actually take AI transformation seriously without increasing AI/R&D budgets up front."
- **Chief of Staff:** "My job is to drive AI upskilling across the organization, and after doing it for 2 years I'm exhausted. Yes there's potential ROI from all of the agentic workflows we're building, but soul and humanity are being sucked out of our processes."
- **Finance leader:** "We acquired a multibillion dollar old school business. Getting that business to be AI-native is incredibly painful largely because people aren't willing to adopt it."
