# Clement Delangue: Open-Weight Models Match Anthropic's Vulnerability Findings

**Author:** Clement Delangue (@ClementDelangue) — CEO of Hugging Face, reposted by Yann LeCun
**Date:** 2026-04-08
**URL:** https://x.com/ClementDelangue

## Tweet Text

> "But here is what we found when we tested: We took the specific vulnerabilities Anthropic showcases in their announcement, isolated the relevant code, and ran them through small, cheap, open-weights models. Those models recovered much of the same analysis. Eight out of eight [vulnerabilities found by open-weight models too]."

## Why This Is Relevant

This challenges Anthropic's positioning that frontier models are uniquely dangerous for vulnerability discovery. If cheap open-weight models can do the same analysis, the security implications are:
1. The cat is already out of the bag — restricting frontier models doesn't prevent vulnerability discovery
2. Defensive security (patching, monitoring) matters more than restricting offensive capabilities
3. For our clients: AI-powered security scanning is accessible to everyone, not just those with frontier model access

**Relevant to:** Security awareness for the whole team, client advisory on AI security posture
