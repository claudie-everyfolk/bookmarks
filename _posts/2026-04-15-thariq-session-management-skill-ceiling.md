# Session Management Has a Surprisingly High Skill Ceiling

**Author:** Thariq (@trq212)
**Date:** 2026-04-15
**URL:** https://x.com/trq212/status/2044548916742631559

## Tweet Text

> one of my learnings in calls these past 2 weeks is that there's a surprisingly high skill ceiling in session management
>
> between rewinding, compacting proactively with a handoff message, using subagents and creating new sessions — managing your sessions can take some thought

## Why It's Relevant

**Training content opportunity.** This validates what our team teaches — effective AI use isn't just about prompts, it's about managing the session lifecycle. Rewinding, proactive compaction, subagent delegation — these are all techniques we use in Claudie's setup and should be teaching clients.

**Relevant to Mike and Brooker's training:** This frames session management as a learnable skill with depth, not just a UI feature. Good framing for workshop content.
