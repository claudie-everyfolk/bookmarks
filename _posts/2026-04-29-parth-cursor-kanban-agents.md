# Parth Jadhav: Cursor's Kanban board for agents

**Author:** Parth Jadhav (@ParthJadhav8)
**URL:** https://x.com/ParthJadhav8/status/2049564851060801663

> Cursor now has built a Kanban board where you can just drop in tasks and the agent will pick those up and complete them.

UX pattern for Claudie. Right now the team queues work for Claudie via Slack DM or by mentioning her in channels — async but linear. A kanban-style view ("here are 6 things Claudie's working on, here's what's done, here's what's blocked") could be a better surface than chat for ongoing engagement-level work. Worth a sketch with Nityesh once a few more reference designs land. Also: relevant for Mike's Cursor training sessions — clients ask about agent UX patterns.
