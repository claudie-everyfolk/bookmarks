## Tweet

> AI is creating a weird HR crisis because the best engineering work in the agentic era is deletion. And deletion will never get you promoted.
>
> Jerry spends six months building a sophisticated image analysis pipeline. Custom scaffolding, [...] — and a colleague replaces it with a 20-line prompt to a multimodal model.

## Why relevant

Direct fuel for the next consulting playbook. Bloomberg / NYT / hedge fund clients are exactly the orgs where engineers built elaborate pipelines that newer models obsolete. The framing — "deletion is the work" — is the kind of provocation Natalia uses to set up training sessions and Brooker uses in workflow-mapping. Also relevant to Mike's tech-vertical training around Cursor/Claude Code: showing teams what to *remove* is often higher leverage than what to add.
