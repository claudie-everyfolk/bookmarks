# vas: you can't automate an operation you haven't audited

**Author:** vas (@vasuman)
**URL:** https://x.com/vasuman/status/2058628503264952611

> And it's exactly why you need an audit before you start any AI transformation. If you don't understand how the operation actually runs today, you have no chance of automating it.

## Why it's relevant
This is literally the Every Consulting pitch — process maps before automation, discovery calls before tooling. Useful quote for sales decks and discovery conversations. Also relevant to the `discovery-to-process-map` and `training-prep` skills: the audit-first framing is now mainstream enough that competing firms are named-checking it.
