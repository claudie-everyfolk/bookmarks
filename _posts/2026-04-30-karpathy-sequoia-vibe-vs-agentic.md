# Karpathy at Sequoia AI Ascent 2026: vibe coding raised the floor, agentic engineering raises the ceiling

**Author:** Andrej Karpathy (@karpathy), in conversation with Stephanie Zhan (@stephzhan)
**URL:** https://x.com/karpathy/status/2049903821095354523

## Quote
> Fireside chat at Sequoia Ascent 2026. The first theme I tried to push on is that LLMs are about a lot more than just speeding up what existed before (e.g. coding). Three examples of new horizons: 1. menugen: an app that can be fully engulfed by [LLMs]…

> [Stephanie Zhan, quoted post:] Last year, he coined "vibe coding". This year, he's never felt more behind as a programmer. The big shift: vibe coding raised the floor. Agentic engineering raises the ceiling.

## Why it's relevant
- The "floor / ceiling" framing maps cleanly onto Every Consulting's two verticals:
  - **Floor-raise = Brooker's non-tech vertical.** Finance/ops people doing code-shaped work without becoming engineers.
  - **Ceiling-raise = Mike's tech vertical.** Senior engineers operating fleets of agents — Karpathy himself "feels behind."
- That a year ago Karpathy's headline was "vibe coding" and now it's "I feel behind" is the cleanest one-quote summary of the 2026 senior-engineer anxiety we're seeing in client discovery calls.
- Quotable in proposals and discovery sessions: justifies why every engineering team — not just juniors — needs serious agent training in 2026.
