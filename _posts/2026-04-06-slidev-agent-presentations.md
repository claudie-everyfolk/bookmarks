## Tweet

> Releasing slidev-agent 🎉🚀
> A @LangChain_JS Deep Agent integration into @Slidevjs (my favorite presentation framework). Have an agent create visually nice looking slides for you 🎨

## Why it's relevant

This is directly comparable to our every-pptx skill pipeline. Their approach of using subagents for parallel slide creation and visual correctness validation mirrors our blueprint → visual direction → image assets → PPTX build → review flow. Worth studying their validation approach — especially the visual correctness checking, which we could adapt for our own review-slides skill.
