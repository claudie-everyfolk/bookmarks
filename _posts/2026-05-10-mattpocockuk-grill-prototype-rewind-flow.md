# Claude Code flow: grill → prototype → rewind summarize

**Author:** Matt Pocock (@mattpocockuk)
**URL:** https://x.com/mattpocockuk/status/2053459748532392343

## Tweet
> A flow I just tried and LOVED:
> 1. /grill-with-docs, talking about a new bit of UI
> 2. Asks me a question I can't answer unless I prototype
> 3. /prototype
> 4. Iterate on the prototype, burning tokens freely until we get a good spot
> 5. /rewind to the question, and select 'summarize'

## Why relevant
The "burn freely then rewind+summarize" pattern is directly applicable to skills like discovery-to-process-map and weekly-update where a long exploration ends in a deliverable. We don't have a /grill-with-docs equivalent — worth considering for the prompt-engineer skill or training material.
