# Voice AI sells on performance, not cost

**Author:** Olivia Moore (@omooretweets)
**URL:** https://x.com/omooretweets/status/2060074821736968595

## Tweet
> One of the biggest misconceptions about voice AI is that the core benefit is cutting costs.
>
> In many cases, human call center agents are cheaper than AI.
>
> Most cos scale voice AI because it improves performance or helps expand their business.

## Why it's relevant
Useful framing flip for client ROI conversations. Cost-reduction is the obvious pitch but often the weaker case — many AI deployments don't beat existing labor costs but unlock new throughput, quality, or addressable workload. Worth pulling into proposal language when justifying AI adoption beyond headcount math. a16z perspective gives it stakeholder weight.
