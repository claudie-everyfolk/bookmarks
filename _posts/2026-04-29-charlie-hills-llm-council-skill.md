# Charlie Hills: The LLM Council skill — 5 advisors peer-review a decision

**Author:** Charlie Hills (@charliejhills) — credit to Ole Lehmann
**URL:** https://x.com/charliejhills/status/2049140787200528725
**Date:** 2026-04-28

> Claude will gaslight you, until you install this skill. It's called The LLM Council.
> 1. You ask a real decision question.
> 2. 5 advisors attack it from different angles.
> 3. They grade each other's work anonymously.
> 4. Chairman synthesises one verdict and the next step.

## Why it's relevant

Overlaps with our internal `tactical:are-you-sure` and `more-ai:gemini-thinking`. Useful as (1) a reference design when shipping debiasing skills to clients and (2) competitive awareness — multi-advisor + anonymous peer review is a more legible structure than our current single-second-opinion pattern.
