## Why it's relevant
This is the #1 objection we hear in enterprise training sessions: "Claude works fine on small repos but not on our actual codebase." A written-down playbook for the large-codebase case is directly useful as:
- A reference we can hand clients between sessions
- Source material to fold into Every's own training curriculum
- Evidence that this is a known pattern with known solutions, not a fundamental blocker
