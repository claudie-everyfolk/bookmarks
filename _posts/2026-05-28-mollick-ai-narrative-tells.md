# Ethan Mollick: AI Has Narrative Tells That Persist Across Style Prompts

**Author:** Ethan Mollick (@emollick)
**URL:** https://x.com/emollick/status/2059851903089930685

## Tweet

> There is a lot being written about the stylistic tells of AI writing (em-dashes, etc.) but this paper looks at AI narrative tells. Fascinating differences between AI & human narrative, and asking AI to write in different styles doesn't do much to change it.

## Why relevant

Affects every "write in voice X" skill we run — including `natalia-email-ghostwriter`, the meeting-followup drafts, and Every's editorial work. If style prompts don't fix narrative tells, the fix has to come from structural changes (sentence order, paragraph length, how arguments are built) — not just diction. Worth reading the underlying arxiv paper and updating the ghostwriter skills.
