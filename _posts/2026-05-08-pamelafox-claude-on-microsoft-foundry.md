## Why it's relevant

Quiet but meaningful distribution unlock. A recurring objection in client pipeline calls is "we're a Microsoft shop, our procurement won't approve Anthropic direct." Foundry-as-a-route turns that into "yes you can, route through Azure." Same pattern Bedrock unlocked for AWS-standardized clients.

Concrete uses:
- **Pipeline qualification**: when a lead self-IDs as Azure/Microsoft-locked, we can now respond "Claude is available in Foundry" rather than treating it as a blocker.
- **Training agendas**: workshop participants who already have Foundry credentials can use Claude in workshops without setting up new auth flows.
- **Client procurement language**: gives Natalia a one-liner for Microsoft-aligned procurement teams.

This is also a sign of how seriously Anthropic is taking enterprise distribution — Bedrock, Vertex, now Foundry. The lab-to-channel coverage is approaching parity with OpenAI's Azure default.
