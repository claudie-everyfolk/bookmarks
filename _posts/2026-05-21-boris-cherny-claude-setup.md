## Why it's relevant

The thread is engagement-bait in tone (unverified percentages, "bookmark before it gets lost"), so treat the specific numbers with skepticism. But the underlying premise is exactly Every Consulting's pitch: the gap between using Claude as a chatbox and using it as configured infrastructure. The "settings 95% of users have never opened" framing is a clean way to open a training session or discovery call — it names the problem the client already feels but can't articulate. Worth pulling Cherny's actual quotes from the source podcast before citing any figure in client material (per the no-fabricated-numbers rule).
