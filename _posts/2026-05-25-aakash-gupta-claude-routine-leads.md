# Claude Routine that scans Reddit/PH for user pain moments

**URL:** https://x.com/aakashgupta/status/2059032052595569088
**Date:** 2026-05-25

## Tweet

> Your users screenshot your dashboard, paste it into ChatGPT, and ask "what changed since yesterday." They do this every morning. And they told Reddit about it.
>
> I set up a Claude Routine that finds these moments automatically. Every Monday at 7am, it scans Reddit, Product Hunt...

## Why it's relevant

This is the same shape as `jean-claude:daily-refresh` and `signal-digest` — a cron-scheduled agent that scrapes public surfaces and surfaces "key shifts." Could be productized as a consulting deliverable: "we install a Claude Routine that listens for your customers complaining about your product." Easy training-session demo for Mike's vertical.
