# Hosted Filesystems for Agents Are the New RAG

**Author:** Jerry Liu (@jerryjliu0) — LlamaIndex founder
**Date:** 2026-04-07
**URL:** https://x.com/jerryjliu0/status/2041326122978418731

## Tweet

> Hosted filesystems for agents are the new RAG

## Why it's relevant

Short but potent take from the LlamaIndex founder. The shift from RAG (retrieve chunks from vector DB) to giving agents direct filesystem access represents a fundamental change in how AI systems interact with knowledge. Instead of retrieval-augmented generation, you give agents a real filesystem to read/write.

This is literally how Claudie works — we have a filesystem (~/bookmarks, ~/discoveries, ~/work) rather than a vector DB. Jerry Liu calling this "the new RAG" validates our architecture. Worth discussing with Nityesh as validation of our approach.
