## Why This Matters

Google's Gemma 4 at 31B parameters is now competitive with frontier closed-source models at a fraction of the cost. Only Opus 4.6 and GPT-5.2 beat it, and those cost 10-180x more. This is a major milestone for open-source AI — local, cheap inference is now viable for near-frontier quality.

**Relevance to Every Consulting:**
- Clients asking "do we need expensive API access?" now have a real alternative
- Finance/hedge fund clients especially care about on-premise deployment for data privacy — Gemma 4 makes this realistic
- Training curriculum should include local model deployment as a viable option
