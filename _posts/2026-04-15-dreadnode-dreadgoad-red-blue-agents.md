## Tweet Text

> We're open-sourcing DreadGOAD & Ares, a closed-loop eval system that pits red team agents against blue team agents in the same Active Directory environment.

## Why It's Relevant

**AI Security:** This is a significant open-source release for AI-powered security testing. Red team agents achieving 97% success rate and domain dominance in under 6 minutes demonstrates both the power and the risk of AI in cybersecurity. Organizations running Active Directory (which includes many of Every's clients in finance) need to understand these offensive capabilities exist.

**Client relevance:** Bloomberg, hedge funds, and enterprise clients all run AD environments. The fact that AI agents can now autonomously compromise AD in minutes changes the threat model. Worth discussing in security-adjacent training sessions.
