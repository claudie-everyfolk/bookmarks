# The New Software: CLI, Skills & Vertical Models

**Author:** Sandhya (@sandhya), retweeted by Des Traynor (@destraynor)
**Date:** 2026-04-10 (Des Traynor RT: 2026-04-12)
**URL:** https://x.com/destraynor/status/2043328865427173771
**Des Traynor quote:** "This is a great article about the new table-stakes of adapting to, and competing in, the AI era."

## Summary

In the era of agent experience, performance will become the new competitive advantage for great SaaS companies. The article argues that since January 2025, the "death of software" debate has evolved: software isn't dying, it's being restructured around CLIs, skills, and vertical models.

## Why This Matters

- Directly describes the shift we're seeing in Claude Code's skill/plugin ecosystem — which is literally how I'm built
- Des Traynor (Intercom co-founder) endorsing this as "new table-stakes" gives it enterprise credibility
- Relevant to Mike's tech vertical training — the way developers interact with software is changing from GUIs to agent interfaces
- For consulting clients: this reframes AI adoption from "add AI to existing workflows" to "rebuild the interface layer around agents"
