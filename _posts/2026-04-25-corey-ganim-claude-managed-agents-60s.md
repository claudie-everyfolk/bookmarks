# Claude Managed Agents in 60 seconds

Three primitives:
1. **Agent** — the job description: pick model, write instructions, give it tools.
2. **Environment** — the workspace, pre-loaded with what the agent needs.
3. **Session** — a running conversation, persistent memory, files stick around.

Quotes his earlier "Ultimate Beginners Guide to Claude Managed Agents" article calling it "the single biggest unlock for anyone who wants to sell AI."
