# Enterprise roadmap vs lab trajectory

**Author:** Ethan Mollick (@emollick)
**URL:** https://x.com/emollick/status/2053816828917359082

## Tweet
> Enterprises are going to actually want a coherent roadmap for the development of tools like Codex and Cowork, so they can plan and train and scale their use. This conflicts with the Labs' vision where these tools rapidly scale exponentially in ability as models approach AGI.

## Why relevant
Names the tension Every absorbs for clients: stable training plans vs. weekly platform shifts. Frame this in pipeline conversations — "you don't need a roadmap, you need a partner who tracks the changes for you."
