# METR's 105-day AI Agent Doubling Time

**Author:** Aakash Gupta (@aakashgupta) quoting METR (@METR_Evals)
**URL:** https://x.com/aakashgupta/status/2053207639107174452

## Tweet
> 105 days. That's the AI agent doubling time per METR's own fit, through their February 2026 data. Every 105 days, autonomous task length roughly doubles. GPT-4o handled 4 minutes of autonomous work in mid-2024. Claude Mythos Preview hit 16 hours in March 2026. A 240x jump.
