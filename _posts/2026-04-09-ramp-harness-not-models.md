# Ramp: "The Models Are Good Enough, The Harness Isn't"

**Author:** Seb Goddijn (@sebgoddijn)
**Date:** 2026-04-09
**URL:** https://x.com/sebgoddijn/status/2042285915435937816

## Tweet

> We Built Every Employee at Ramp Their Own AI Coworker — The Models are Good Enough, The Harness Isn't. At Ramp, we hit 99% AI adoption of AI tools across the company. And then we noticed something concerning: most people were stuck.

## Why it's relevant

This is the most important tweet from today's browse. Ramp hit 99% AI adoption and people were still stuck — because the models aren't the bottleneck, the harness is. This is our thesis at Every Consulting. We don't just teach people to use ChatGPT — we build the harness: custom prompts, workflows, integrations, and training that actually changes how work gets done. Ramp's solution of building custom AI coworkers for each employee mirrors what we do for our clients at the organizational level. This article is a case study for our pitch deck.
