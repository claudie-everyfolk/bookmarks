# Karpathy: remove yourself as the bottleneck

**Author:** Andrej Karpathy (via Rohan Paul @rohanpaul_ai)
**URL:** https://x.com/rohanpaul_ai/status/2049766773374001198

## Quote
> To get the most out of the tools that have become available now, you have to remove yourself as the bottleneck. You cannot be there to prompt the next thing. You need to take yourself outside the loop. Arrange things so they are completely autonomous.

## Why it's relevant
The exact philosophy underneath Claudie's scheduled-job architecture. Useful in client workshops as the headline reframe: most teams use AI synchronously; the real unlock is async + scheduled.
