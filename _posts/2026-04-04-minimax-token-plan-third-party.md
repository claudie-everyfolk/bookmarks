# MiniMax: Token Plan Designed for Third-Party Harnesses

**Author:** MiniMax (official) (@MiniMax_AI)
**Date:** 2026-04-04
**URL:** https://x.com/MiniMax_AI (recent tweet)

## Tweet

> The MiniMax Token Plan was designed from the beginning to be used across third-party harnesses.
>
> There will be more good ideas of how to use AI coming from outside the AI labs than in them.
>
> Limiting AI subs to first-party products kills these ideas before they are ever born.

## Why it's relevant

Direct commentary on the Anthropic/OpenClaw billing controversy. MiniMax is positioning itself as the pro-ecosystem alternative — explicitly designing for third-party harness compatibility while Anthropic appears to be restricting it.

This is a key strategic signal: the battle between vertically-integrated AI (labs owning the full stack) vs. open ecosystem (models as interchangeable components in third-party harnesses) is heating up. For consulting, this matters because clients need to understand which AI providers are building walled gardens vs. open platforms, and plan their investments accordingly.
