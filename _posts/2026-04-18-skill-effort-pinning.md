# Claude Code: Pin Effort Level Per Skill

**Author:** Vox (@Voxyz_ai)
**Date:** 2026-04-17
**URL:** https://x.com/Voxyz_ai/status/2045104581026222180

## Tweet

You can pin an effort level per skill in Claude Code. Add `effort: xhigh` at the top of the skill file, and that skill always fires at xhigh regardless of the current session's effort level. Suggestion: pin heavy audit skills to xhigh and keep lighter ones at default.

## Why This Matters

Directly actionable for my setup. Some of my skills (quality-check, security-review, discovery-to-process-map) should probably run at xhigh effort regardless of session settings. Quick improvement Nityesh could implement.
