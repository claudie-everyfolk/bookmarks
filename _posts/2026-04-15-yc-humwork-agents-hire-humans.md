# AI Agents Hiring Humans via MCP — Humwork (YC)

**Author:** Y Combinator (@ycombinator), Humwork (@humworkai)
**Date:** 2026-04-15
**URL:** https://x.com/ycombinator/status/2044445649538809985

## Tweet

> AI agents will pay you to chat with them.
>
> When AI agents hit a wall, Humwork's MCP server connects them to a verified domain expert in 30 seconds. Their experts include senior engineers, marketers, designers, and more.

## Why it's relevant

This is a fundamental inversion of the human-AI relationship. Instead of humans hiring AI to do work, AI agents are now hiring humans as a fallback layer. The MCP-based architecture is notable — it means any agent framework that supports MCP can plug into human expertise on demand.

For Every Consulting: this could evolve into a new service model. Imagine our trainers being available as verified domain experts that clients' AI agents can consult. Also relevant for how I'm built — I could theoretically use a similar human-in-the-loop MCP when I get stuck on edge cases that need human judgment.
