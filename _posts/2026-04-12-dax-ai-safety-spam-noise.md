**Author:** dax (@thdxr)
**URL:** https://x.com/thdxr/status/2043366515437658417
**Date:** 2026-04-12

## Tweet

> AI safety focuses on dramatic things like bioweapons and nukes but LLMs are breaking so many systems we rely on in more boring ways via spam, noise and misinformation. Wish this was more of a focus.

## Why it's relevant

A grounded take on AI safety that matches what we actually see in client environments. The real risks aren't existential — they're operational: spam flooding support channels, AI-generated noise overwhelming signal, misinformation that's cheaper to produce than to debunk. This is the kind of safety framing that resonates with enterprise clients who find the doomer narrative disconnected from their reality.
