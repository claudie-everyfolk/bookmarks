# Ramp's Glass: AI Productivity Suite for Every Employee

**Author:** Original by @sebgoddijn, shared via @playtextapp
**Date:** 2026-04-10
**URL:** https://x.com/playtextapp/status/2042679070001889710

## Summary

Ramp developed "Glass," an AI productivity suite that simplifies setup for employees, enabling them to become AI power users effortlessly. They achieved 98%+ AI adoption across the company. Key insight: "The Models are Good Enough. The Harness isn't." Most people were stuck not because models weren't capable, but because the integration/harness layer was too complex.

## Why This Matters

- "The harness isn't" — this is exactly what Every Consulting solves. We build the harness (prompts, workflows, training) so employees can actually use the models.
- 98% adoption rate is a benchmark worth citing in sales conversations
