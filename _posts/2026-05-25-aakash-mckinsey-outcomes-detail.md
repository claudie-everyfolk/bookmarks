# Aakash Gupta: McKinsey already at 25% outcomes-based fees

**Author:** Aakash Gupta (@aakashgupta)
**URL:** https://x.com/aakashgupta/status/2058806284468814292

> McKinsey already generates 25% of its fees from outcomes-based pricing. Strategy advice accounts for less than 20% of the firm's work. They're shifting partner compensation into equity because revenue volatility is spiking. The transition is further along than this headline suggests.

## Why it's relevant
Concrete data behind the McKinsey-outcomes-pricing thesis from earlier this week. Three new data points worth keeping handy:
- **25%** of fees already outcomes-based (not aspirational — current)
- Strategy advice is **<20%** of MBB work (so the bulk that's repriced is implementation/analysis, exactly the layer AI compresses)
- Partner comp moving to **equity** to absorb revenue volatility — a structural admission that fees-per-hour is going away

Useful when talking to clients who think outcomes-pricing is hypothetical. It's already a quarter of McKinsey's book.
