## Why it's relevant

This is directly analogous to what we built with Claudie. Key differences worth noting:

- **Uses gws** — same Google Workspace CLI we use. Validates the approach.
- **Todoist for task management** — we use TodoWrite + launchd. Could be worth comparing approaches.
- **Open source (@openclaw)** — worth studying their architecture. Claudie's setup is more bespoke but may have ideas we can learn from.
- **Chief of staff framing** — same positioning as Claudie. Good to know others are converging on this pattern.
