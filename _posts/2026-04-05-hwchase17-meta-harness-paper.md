## Why This Is Relevant

"Learning at the harness layer" is exactly what we're doing with Claudie — the intelligence isn't just in the model, it's in the infrastructure around it (memory files, scheduled jobs, skill system, tool orchestration). This paper likely formalizes patterns we're using intuitively. Combined with the Anthropic harness design article from today's feed, there's a clear trend: the industry is recognizing that agent infrastructure matters as much as the underlying model.
