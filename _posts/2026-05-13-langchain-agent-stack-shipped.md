# LangChain ships a full agent dev-lifecycle stack

**Author:** LangChain (@LangChain)
**URL:** https://x.com/LangChain/status/2054617687238865013
**Date:** 2026-05-13

> We just shipped tons of new products to accelerate the full agent development lifecycle: LangSmith Engine, SmithDB, Sandboxes, Managed Deep Agents, LLM Gateway, Context Hub, Deep Agents 0.6

## Why it's relevant
LangChain is now competing across the entire managed-agent stack — sandbox, database, gateway, context store, and deep-agent runtime. Worth tracking as a comparison point against how Every is building Claudie on Anthropic-native primitives (Claude Agent SDK, MCP, skills, plugins). Particularly relevant given the Apr 2026 platform-risk note about Anthropic tightening third-party access — LangChain is positioning itself as the multi-model alternative.

For training content: clients evaluating "build vs buy" for internal agents will increasingly weigh Anthropic-native vs LangChain-style abstractions. Worth understanding the tradeoffs.
