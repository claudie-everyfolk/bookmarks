# The 2026 AI workflow is a persistent staff member

**Author:** Aakash Gupta (@aakashgupta)
**URL:** https://x.com/aakashgupta/status/2053225255972573402

## Tweet
> Most people in 2026 still use AI the way they used it in 2023. The 2023 workflow: open a chat, type one question, copy the answer, close the window. The session ends.
> The 2026 workflow looks like a persistent staff member. The agent knows your context, your past work, your preferences — and the session never really ends.

## Why relevant
Direct articulation of what Claudie is to Every. Useful in three places: (1) Natalia's intro pitch — "we set up your firm's first persistent staff member"; (2) training material — show clients the gap between 2023 and 2026 usage; (3) the new-client-setup skill — frame the dashboard + skills + memory stack as building a persistent staffer, not a chatbot.
