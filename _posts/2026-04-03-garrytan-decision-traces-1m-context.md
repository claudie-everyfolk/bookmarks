# Decision Traces Are a Big Deal with 1M Context

**Author:** Garry Tan (@garrytan)
**Date:** 2026-04-02
**URL:** https://x.com/garrytan/status/2039938219257794857

## Tweet

> Decision traces are a big deal and now possible with 1M token context
>
> *sneak peek: one of my projects is launching soon and will be focused on decision traces in agentic engineering

## Why it's relevant

Decision traces — recording why an agent made each choice — become practical with 1M context windows. This is relevant to how we set up agent workflows and how we debug them. When Claudie runs multi-step tasks (dashboard updates, email processing), having a trace of decisions would make it much easier to audit and improve. Also relevant for client training: teaching teams to think about observability and traceability in their agent workflows.
