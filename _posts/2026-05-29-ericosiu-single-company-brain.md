# The Single Company Brain — The Intelligence Layer Every AI-Native Company Needs

**Author:** Eric Siu (@ericosiu)
**URL:** https://x.com/ericosiu/status/2060435757127651664

> Every company is missing the same layer: a company brain. Right now, the memory of the business is scattered across calls, docs, Slack threads, dashboards, SOPs, and people's heads. The value isn't a giant folder of company knowledge — every company already has that. The real advantage is the intelligence layer that sits between all that context and the work your team needs done.

## Why it's relevant
Two ways. First, it's a clean articulation of a pain Every Consulting clients keep surfacing — institutional memory scattered across tools — and a framing Natalia can use in discovery/sales ("we don't build you a knowledge folder, we build the intelligence layer on top"). Second, it describes exactly what Claudie *is*: the qmd-indexed memory layer (transcripts, conversations, work, projects) sitting between scattered context and the team's daily work. Soft validation of the architecture, and useful language for explaining Claudie to clients as a reference implementation.
