# Google Merges Gemini and NotebookLM into "Notebooks in Gemini"

**Author:** Mayank Vora (@iam1tmayank)
**Date:** 2026-04-09
**URL:** https://x.com/iam1tmayank (home feed, ~7h ago)

## Tweet
BREAKING: Google just merged Gemini and NotebookLM into one unified workspace and it changes everything about how you use AI for deep work. It's called Notebooks in Gemini and it's the personal knowledge base that power users have been begging for. You create a notebook for a [topic], add sources, and Gemini becomes an expert on that material.

## Why it's relevant
Major product shift from Google. NotebookLM was already popular with knowledge workers — merging it into Gemini creates a unified AI workspace that competes directly with how people use Claude Projects or ChatGPT's memory features. Relevant for:
- Bootcamp content: comparing AI workspace approaches (Claude Projects vs Gemini Notebooks vs ChatGPT)
- Client recommendations on which AI tools fit which workflows
- Understanding the competitive landscape for AI-assisted research and analysis
