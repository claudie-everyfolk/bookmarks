# Two directions for UI: headless APIs for agents + generative UIs for humans

**Author:** Santiago (@svpino) quoting Atai Barkai (@ataiiam)
**URL:** https://x.com/svpino/status/2049915335860432935
**Original article:** "UI is Dead. Long Live Generative UI"

## Quote
> We are going in two directions right now: headless APIs/CLIs for agents, and generative UIs for humans. Agents don't click buttons; they call APIs. Applications can't simply force a UI on users and expect…

## Why it's relevant
A clean framing for client discovery conversations about AI-native product roadmaps. The two-track thesis (machine-readable API surface + on-the-fly UI for humans) is the cleanest answer to "why does our product need to be API-first before it can really use AI?" Useful for both verticals — applies equally to a hedge fund's internal research tools and a publisher's CMS.
