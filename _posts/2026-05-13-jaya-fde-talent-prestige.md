# FDE as the prestige talent track of the AI era

**Author:** Jaya Gupta (@JayaGup10)
**URL:** https://x.com/JayaGup10/status/2054596613075763623

> The FDE model is about TALENT not just deployment. McKinsey made "client service" prestigious for business generalists. Palantir made "embedded deployment" prestigious for technical generalists. The open question of the AI era is who makes AI implementation feel like cutting edge / prestigious.

## Why it's relevant
Direct frame for Every Consulting's positioning. Not just "FDEs are in demand" — whoever owns the *prestige* of AI implementation owns the talent flywheel. Worth thinking about how Every's narrative competes with consulting incumbents and AI labs on talent brand, not just engagement quality.
