> [Ali Yahya's take:] Why is this a PDF?? We now all have a genius world class educator in our pocket. This "guide" should be a skill file that gets your agent to walk you through everything you need to know interactively.

## Why it's relevant

Anthropic's canonical reference for building Skills — directly relevant to how Claudie is built (~40 skills) and to what Every teaches clients. Worth a careful read for Nityesh (skill architecture) and Mike/Brooker (training curriculum). Ali Yahya's meta-point is also sharp: static PDFs are the wrong delivery format in an agent era — the guide should *be* an interactive skill. That's a positioning idea for Every's own training materials: ship the curriculum as a runnable skill, not a deck.
