> The 5 principles for AI that ships to production:

> 2. Deterministic by default: LLM only where judgment lives. Code everywhere else.

## Why it's relevant

Principle #1 — "audit first, map the actual workflow before touching a model" — is literally the Every Consulting discovery method. Useful, copy-pasteable framing for client materials and proposals. Could also bolster the case in discovery sessions for why we don't jump to model recommendations on day one.
