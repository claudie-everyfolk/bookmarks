# The Single Company Brain — one AI operating layer across the stack

## Tweet

> How we built a Single Company Brain (and how you can too). Your company already has a brain — it's just scattered across Slack, Gong, HubSpot, and someone who's out today. We connect agents, Slack, GA4, HubSpot, Salesforce, Gong, Search Console into one AI operating layer.

## Why it's relevant

A productized version of what Claudie already is for Every Consulting — a single brain wired into Slack, Google Workspace, Attio, Granola, qmd. Useful as both a competitive reference point and a client-facing concept: many clients want exactly this. Worth studying Siu's framing to sharpen how we pitch 'connect your scattered context into one agent.'

## Concrete takeaways

- This is the consumer/SMB framing of the enterprise context problem Levie names — same thesis, different audience.
- Claudie is a working proof-of-concept of the pattern. The teammate-facing pitch writes itself: 'we built this for ourselves; here's how it works.'
- Caution: Siu's version connects sales/marketing stacks (HubSpot, Gong, GA4). Our consulting clients (finance, media) have different source systems — the architecture transfers, the connectors don't.
