# Aaron Levie: token costs are the #1 heated topic among Fortune 500 CIOs

**Author:** Aaron Levie (@levie) — CEO of Box
**URL:** https://x.com/levie/status/2056965292753146019

> Token costs will become a dominant topic in enterprises going forward with AI. Just got out of a dinner with many Fortune 500 enterprise CIOs and this was the most heated topic. A mix of strategies are being employed, but basically no one feels like they have the right answer.

Posted as a quote-tweet of OpenAI's "Guaranteed Capacity" announcement — an offering letting customers lock in long-term OpenAI compute access.

## Why it's relevant

Token cost is shifting from an engineering footnote to a board-level CIO concern. For Every Consulting this is fresh sales context: the conversation with clients is moving from "adopt AI" to "adopt AI without losing budget control." It directly supports a proposed lightweight "AI Tool Spend Audit" SKU and an agent cost-economics training module.
