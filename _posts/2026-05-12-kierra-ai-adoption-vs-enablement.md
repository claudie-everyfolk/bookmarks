> AI adoption vs actual AI enablement
>
> Adoption ensures tools/resources are purchased. Enablement ensures you have a strategy in plan to build the training, culture, and infrastructure that allows employees to successfully + sustainably extract business value from said tools.

Quoted Joe Weisenthal on FT reporting Amazon employees automating random pointless tasks to burn tokens and look productive — the canonical adoption-without-enablement failure mode.

## Why it's relevant

This is the exact wedge our consulting practice cuts. Two-line framing fits cold emails, deck intros, and the re-engagement angle for clients who licensed Copilot/ChatGPT Enterprise but never built the actual program. Add to natalia-email-ghostwriter snippets.
