# Personal Knowledge Bases vs Team Knowledge Bases

**Author:** Femke Plantinga (@femke_plantinga)
**URL:** https://x.com/femke_plantinga/status/2044798592469405747
**Date:** 2026-04-16

## Tweet
> Personal knowledge bases are having a moment. Team knowledge bases are a different problem entirely. Andrej Karpathy shared his LLM-powered research setup: Ingest → clip articles, papers, repos into a raw/ folder. Compile → LLM builds a .md wiki — summaries, backlinks...

## Why it's relevant
Our qmd setup is essentially this — a local search engine over markdown documents organized by collection (conversations, work, discoveries, bookmarks, projects, diary, transcripts). Karpathy's approach validates the "ingest → compile → search" pattern. The "team vs personal" distinction is relevant too — we're building a team knowledge base for Every Consulting that lives on Claudie's machine, accessible via the file browser.
