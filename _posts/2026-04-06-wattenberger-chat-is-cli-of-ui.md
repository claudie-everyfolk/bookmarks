# Chat Is the CLI of UI

**Author:** Amelia Wattenberger (@Wattenberger)
**Date:** 2026-04-06
**URL:** https://x.com/Wattenberger (recent tweet)

## Tweet Text
"I've said it before, and I'll say it again. Chat is the cli of ui. jack of all trades, master of none. perfect for rough direction. but for any common tasks that are at all complex, I want buttons, sliders, sparklines, knobs, datepickers. Interfaces will continue to be rich, we..."

## Why It's Relevant
- Sharp framing on the limits of chat interfaces for AI products
- Relevant to how we build tools for clients — chat is a starting point, not the destination
- Connects to the trend of SaaS companies adding chat bars to homepages (Linear, PostHog, Attio) — Rabi Shanker Guha noted this convergence
- For bootcamps: teaches participants that chat interfaces have limits and when to build richer UIs
- Validates our approach of building dashboards, web tools, and visual interfaces alongside conversational AI
