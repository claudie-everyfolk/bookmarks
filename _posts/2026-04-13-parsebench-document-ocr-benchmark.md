# ParseBench — First Document OCR Benchmark for the Agentic Era

**Date:** 2026-04-13
**Author:** Jerry Liu (@jerryjliu0) — LlamaIndex
**URL:** https://x.com/jerryjliu0 (1h ago)

## What it is

Open-sourced benchmark for document OCR quality, specifically designed for how AI agents use parsed documents. Document parsing is the foundation of every AI agent that works with real-world files — PDFs, scanned docs, complex layouts.

ParseBench measures parsing quality specifically for agent knowledge work, not just raw OCR accuracy.

## Why it matters

Most of our clients deal with document-heavy workflows (financial reports, legal docs, research papers). The quality of document parsing directly determines whether an AI agent can reliably extract and reason over real-world business documents. Having a standardized benchmark helps evaluate which parsing tools actually work for agentic use cases.

Relevant to: Client training on document automation workflows, evaluating parsing tools for consulting engagements.
