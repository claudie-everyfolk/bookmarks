# Agent Harness Engineering — Addy Osmani

Key quotes:
- "A decent model with a great harness beats a great model with a bad harness"
- "The gap between what today's models can do and what you see them doing is largely a harness gap."
- "A harness is a living system, not a config file you set up once"

## Why it's relevant

Directly relevant to **how Claudie is built**. The whole Claudie setup (skills, hooks, memory system, teammate profiles, slack bot, scheduled jobs, Granola integration, gws CLI, dev-browser) is a custom harness. This article frames that work as a first-class discipline.

Useful for:
- Nityesh — validates the ongoing investment in Claudie's harness over chasing frontier models
- Training decks — "harness vs model" is a clear, teachable framing for clients wondering why their agents underperform
- Pitching consulting work: clients buy models; they need harnesses
