# 60-80% of Anthropic Projects Start Without a PRD

**Author:** Adi Polak (@AdiPolak)
**Date:** 2026-04-09
**URL:** https://x.com/AdiPolak (home feed, ~8h ago)

## Tweet
60-80% of Anthropic projects start without a PRD. Just Slack, context, fast pushback. No heavy docs. Makes me wonder if "good process" is just slow feedback in disguise.

## Why it's relevant
Fascinating insight into how Anthropic actually builds. Directly relevant to our consulting work — when clients ask about "best practices" for AI project management, the answer from the company that builds Claude is: skip the PRD, use fast feedback loops in Slack. This validates the lightweight, iteration-focused approach we advocate in bootcamps. Also relevant to how our own team operates.
