# Medvi: $1.8B on Track with 2 Employees

**Author:** The Startup Ideas Podcast (@startupideasod)
**Date:** 2026-04-03
**URL:** https://x.com/startupideasod (feed post, ~23h ago)

## Tweet
"Sam Altman predicted the first one-person billion-dollar company. Matthew Gallagher built a $401M company in year one with $20,000, AI tools, and zero employees. This year he's on track for $1.8B. With 2 people. The playbook has changed."

Matthew Gallagher used AI to write code, produce website copy, generate images and videos for ads, and handle customer service. His startup Medvi, a telehealth provider of GLP-1 weight-loss drugs, got 300 customers in its first month, gained 1,000 more the second month, and generated $401M in its first full year.

## Why it's relevant
The ultimate case study for "AI changes the economics of business." Directly relevant to Every Consulting's pitch to clients — AI isn't just productivity enhancement, it's a structural shift in what's possible with small teams.

Key talking point for sales conversations: "A $1.8B company runs with 2 people. What could your team of 50 do if they were AI-augmented?"
