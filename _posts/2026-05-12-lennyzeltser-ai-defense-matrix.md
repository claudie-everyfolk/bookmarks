# Lenny Zeltser — AI Defense Matrix

> Making Sense of Security for AI: The AI Defense Matrix maps eight AI asset classes to NIST CSF functions, giving security leaders one grid to assign ownership, find gaps, and select controls. Sounil Yu and I co-authored it.

## Why relevant
A structured grid mapping AI asset classes (models, agents, data, prompts, etc.) to NIST CSF functions (Identify/Protect/Detect/Respond/Recover). This is exactly the kind of artifact Every Consulting clients in regulated industries ask about when AI adoption hits security/compliance walls. Use as a reference frame in security-related discovery calls and training sessions.
