## Tweet

> Hannah Stulberg, a PM at DoorDash, built a shared repo where her team checks in every customer call summary, decision log, and analytics query. Last week a new engineer needed context on a customer decision from three months ago. Instead of pinging Hannah, the agent answered from the repo.

## Why it's relevant

Mirrors Every Consulting's `~/work/` + `~/projects/` setup. Concrete external story Mike could use in Tech-vertical training as social proof for clients hesitant about the "check everything in" discipline.
