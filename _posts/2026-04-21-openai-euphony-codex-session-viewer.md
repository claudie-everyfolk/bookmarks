# Euphony: open-source chat/Codex session log visualizer

> Paste in a public URL or upload a local file, and Euphony turns the raw data into an easy-to-browse view. It supports translation, filtering, editing, and more.

## Why relevant
Claudie already has a job-dashboard for sessions, but Euphony-style format for Codex/OpenAI parity could be useful when clients compare stacks, and the UX ideas (filter, edit, translate) are worth stealing for our own transcript-review tooling.
