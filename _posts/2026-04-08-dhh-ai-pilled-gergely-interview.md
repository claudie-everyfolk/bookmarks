# DHH is Now "Officially AI-Pilled" — Gergely Orosz Interview

**Author:** ℏεsam (@Hesamation), quoting Gergely Orosz (@GergelyOrosz)
**Date:** 2026-04-08
**URL:** https://x.com/Hesamation (quote of @GergelyOrosz interview)

## Tweet
> "What I failed to realize was that running agents feels less like a project manager for agents and more like stepping into a super mech suit where I don't have two arms, I have twelve."
> THE LEGENDARY DHH IS NOW OFFICIALLY AI-PILLED

DHH (creator of Ruby on Rails, 37signals/Basecamp CTO) said just six months ago he doesn't really use AI tools to write code because they aren't good enough. Things have changed — dramatically.

## Why it's relevant
- **Consulting gold:** When a prominent skeptic like DHH publicly converts, it's a powerful data point for client conversations. Many of our clients are in the "it's not good enough" camp. DHH's shift — and his specific framing of agents as a "mech suit" — is an excellent metaphor for training sessions.
- **The "mech suit" framing:** This is better than "copilot" or "assistant." It captures the feeling of amplified capability without loss of control. Worth incorporating into how we talk about AI adoption.
- **Mike's vertical:** Directly relevant for developer-audience training. DHH is a hero to a whole generation of web developers.
