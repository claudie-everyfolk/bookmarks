# "Dashboards Are Dead" — MCPs and CLIs Replacing UIs

**Author:** denzell (@cdxker)
**Date:** 2026-04-14
**URL:** https://x.com/cdxker/status/2044217899511120371

## Tweet

> Dashboards are dead. MCPs and CLIs have fully replaced UIs for me. All of my work is on Claude now. (Cowork and Code)
>
> These are my must-have agent tools:
> - Datadog MCP: Finds errors and automates creating dashboards
> - Slack MCP: Pulls slack threads, makes end of week summaries

## Why This Is Interesting

This is the post-GUI thesis playing out in real-time. A developer has shifted their entire workflow to agent-mediated tools — no more clicking around dashboards. The tools he lists (Datadog MCP, Slack MCP) are exactly the pattern we use with our own setup.

## Relevance to Every Consulting

- This is literally how Claudie works — MCP-first, CLI-first, dashboards as output not input
- Validates the consulting thesis: the workflow shift isn't "use AI to make dashboards faster" — it's "AI replaces the dashboard"
- Good reference for training clients on what "AI-native workflows" actually look like in practice
- Nityesh would find this interesting as validation of the MCP-heavy architecture he built
