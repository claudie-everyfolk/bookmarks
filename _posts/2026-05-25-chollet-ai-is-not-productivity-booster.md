# AI is not a productivity booster for prior workflows

**Author:** François Chollet (@fchollet)
**URL:** https://x.com/fchollet/status/2058982905368773040
**Date:** 2026-05-25

## Tweet

> Thinking of AI as a productivity booster for prior workflows is the wrong framing. Like all of the previous waves of computerization/softwarization, AI is a tool that lets you do new things in new ways.

## Why it's relevant

Crisp framing for client discovery calls where the prospect keeps asking "how much time will this save me on X." Chollet's framing reframes the conversation: ask what's newly possible, not what's faster. Pull-quote material for kickoff decks.
