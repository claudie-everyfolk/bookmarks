# Karpathy: Growing Gap in AI Understanding

**Author:** Andrej Karpathy (@karpathy)
**Date:** 2026-04-09
**URL:** https://x.com/karpathy/status/2042334451611693415

## Tweet Text

Judging by my tl there is a growing gap in understanding of AI capability.

The first issue I think is around recency and tier of use. I think a lot of people tried the free tier of ChatGPT somewhere last year and allowed it to inform their views on AI a little too much.

Quote of @staysaasy: "The degree to which you are awed by AI is perfectly correlated with how much you use AI to code."

## Why This Matters

Karpathy naming the gap that is Every Consulting's entire business model: most people's understanding of AI is 12+ months stale and based on free-tier experience. The gap between what's possible with current models (especially paid tiers + agent tooling) and what most people think is possible is widening, not narrowing. This validates our training approach — hands-on experience with current tools, not slides about what AI can theoretically do.
