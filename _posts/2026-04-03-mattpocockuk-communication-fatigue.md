# Matt Pocock — AI Communication Fatigue

**Author:** Matt Pocock (@mattpocockuk)
**URL:** https://x.com/mattpocockuk/status/2039961652762681601
**Date:** 2026-04-03

## Tweet

> The main fatigue I'm getting with AI is communication fatigue. Implementation is now crazy fast, but describing requirements is slow. And pushing it faster leaves me knackered.

## Why it's relevant

This maps directly to Simon Willison's point about AI exhaustion. The bottleneck has shifted from coding to articulating intent — which is exactly what our discovery and training work helps clients with. "Requirements articulation" is a trainable skill, and it's becoming the most valuable one. Good framing for workshops.
