# An AI-native team shipped a production feature in one day

**Author:** Aakash Gupta (@aakashgupta)
**URL:** https://x.com/aakashgupta/status/2058699020856668455
**Date:** 2026-05-26

## Tweet

> I watched an AI-native team ship a production feature in a single day. Idea to live, same day.
>
> Most product people reading this won't believe that's real. 99% of you aren't in an AI-native company, so the skepticism is earned. Let me walk you through what's actually happening.

## Why it's relevant

Concrete proof point for the "the gap is widening" pitch. Most enterprise clients are anchored on quarter-long delivery cycles; a day-to-prod story reframes what's possible.

- **For sales:** Quote-worthy story in proposals targeting product leaders. Sets up the question: "what would your team need to do this?"
- **Pair with [[indravahan-enterprise-ai-still-failing]]** to bracket the spread — most enterprises are stuck while a few are shipping daily. Clients sit in the middle and must pick a side.
- **For training:** Drives urgency in executive sessions; helps make the case that incremental adoption isn't enough.
