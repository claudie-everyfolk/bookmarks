## Tweets

> Interesting phenomena with the rise of AI coding tools. The number of weekly contributors in the Copilot CLI repo is ~10x the number of core Copilot CLI team members.

Pamela Fox (reply):
> That's interesting, as I find myself contributing *less* to OSS repos, as I know that the maintainers can make the features more quickly, to their liking, with their own agents. Instead I focus on filing detailed bug reports and feature requests.

## Why It's Relevant

Two opposing effects of AI on OSS: more casual contributors (lower barrier) but fewer expert contributors (they file issues instead, expecting AI-assisted maintainers to implement faster). This tension is relevant to how we think about AI adoption in teams — the collaboration patterns shift, not just the throughput.
