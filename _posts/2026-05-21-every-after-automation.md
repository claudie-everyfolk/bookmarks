# After Automation — Dan Shipper on why automating everything created more human work

**Author:** Dan Shipper (@danshipper) — CEO of Every (Every Consulting's parent company)
**Tweet:** https://x.com/danshipper/status/2057514494960513272

> We've automated every single thing we can @every with AI agents. And yet there's way more human work to do than ever. We've gone from 4 -> 30 human employees since GPT-3. I wrote a report on the structural reasons: how AI makes expert competence cheap, why that drives up demand for experts, and why the dynamic only intensifies as we approach AGI.
