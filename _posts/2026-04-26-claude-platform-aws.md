# Claude Platform on AWS

"AWS just announced Claude Platform on AWS. No Bedrock required!! You get Anthropic's native Claude experience directly in your AWS account! IAM handles access, billing consolidated, CloudTrail logs everything alongside other services."

## Why it's relevant

Big enterprise signal. Several Every Consulting clients (Bloomberg, finance firms) are AWS-heavy and have so far had to choose between Bedrock-Claude (lagging features) or direct Anthropic API (procurement friction). Native Claude in AWS removes both. Expect this to come up in client convos in coming weeks — Mike and Natalia should know.
