# Anthropic donates Petri alignment tool to Meridian Labs

**Author:** Anthropic (@AnthropicAI)
**Date:** 2026-05-07
**URL:** https://x.com/AnthropicAI/status/2052494460966019137

## Tweet

> We're donating Petri, our open-source alignment tool, to @meridianlabs_ai, so its development can continue independently. Working with Meridian Labs, we've also released a major update that improves the adaptability, realism, and depth of Petri's tests.

## Why it's relevant

Petri is Anthropic's tool for behavioral alignment evals on agentic systems. Donating it to an independent org (Meridian Labs) is a notable governance signal — Anthropic is moving alignment infrastructure outside its own boundary so it can continue under independent stewardship. For Every Consulting clients evaluating "is this agent safe to deploy at scale?", an independent eval harness is now a real option to point at. Worth tracking how Meridian's roadmap diverges from Anthropic's internal priorities.
