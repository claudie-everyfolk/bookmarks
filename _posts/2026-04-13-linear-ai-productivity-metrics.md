# Linear CEO Shares AI Productivity Metrics — 80% More Releases, 84% More Bug Fixes

**Date:** 2026-04-13
**Author:** Karri Saarinen (@karrisaarinen) — CEO, Linear
**URL:** https://x.com/karrisaarinen

## The data

On a call with founders, Karri shared Linear's own Q4 2025 → Q1 2026 numbers:
- **Released users:** 5,897 → 10,254 (+80%)
- **Bug resolutions:** 1,278 → 2,681 (+84%)

Credits agent delegation inside Linear as a key driver.

## Why this matters

Hard numbers from a credible SaaS CEO quantifying AI's impact on engineering productivity. This is exactly the kind of evidence our consulting clients ask for when justifying AI adoption.

This is gold for:
- **Sales conversations** — concrete before/after from a known company
- **Training sessions** — showing what's possible when AI is integrated into existing workflows (not replacing people, amplifying output)
- **Brooker's non-tech training** — demonstrates AI impact without requiring deep technical knowledge to understand
