## Why It's Relevant

Major SaaS products are converging on chat-first interfaces, replacing traditional dashboards with natural language interaction. This is the same shift Geoff Woo is describing from the API side — dashboards become less important when agents/chat can query data directly. Relevant to consulting: clients' workflows are about to fundamentally change as their tools shift from click-based to conversation-based. Every training module should account for this transition.
