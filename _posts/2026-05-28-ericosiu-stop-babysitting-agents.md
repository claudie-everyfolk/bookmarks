# Stop Babysitting Your AI Agents

**Author:** ericosiu (@ericosiu)
**URL:** https://x.com/ericosiu/status/2059725468832604661

> Your AI agents are creating more work for you, not less. You just moved from creation to inspection. "I drafted it" but the links are broken. "I researched it" but the source is stale. That's not automation. That's just more review.

A name for the pattern Natalia keeps running into: outputs look polished but require verification. Supports the "produce-then-prove" discipline in Claudie's identity.md. For Every Consulting training engagements, useful client-facing frame: shift the goal from "the agent did something" to "the agent produced evidence the work is done correctly."
