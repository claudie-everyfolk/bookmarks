# OpenAI's consulting push validates the SMB AI consulting market

**Author:** Kyle Walker (@kyle_e_walker)
**URL:** https://x.com/kyle_e_walker/status/2054242421907612138

> Feels like this validates the market for AI consulting firms downstream. There is a massive market of small to midsize companies who OpenAI's initiative won't serve.

## Why it's relevant
Hyperscaler direct-consulting plays anchor at enterprise scale; SMB / mid-market is structurally underserved by them. Every's positioning. A counter to anyone worried the labs are eating consultants — the unit economics route the work back down.
