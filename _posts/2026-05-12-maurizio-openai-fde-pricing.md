## Tweet

> I hear clients have to pay $10K/day for OpenAI consultants (or "Forward Deployed Engineers", same thing) with little value out of them to justify such an exorbitant bill.
>
> It's a bit like when you hire SAP services consultants to implement the SAP product when you could have got experts from some SAP partner firm for 1/3 of the cost.
>
> Clients usually start with that configuration because they believe these consultants have better ties to the product arm, then are forced to switch because nobody has infinite budget and the "laziness" of these services folks from the product company become apparent after a few months.

Quoting the OpenAI Deployment Company launch (May 11, 2026) — 19 partner firms, majority owned and controlled by OpenAI.
