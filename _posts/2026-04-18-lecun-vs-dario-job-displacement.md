# Yann LeCun Pushes Back on Dario Amodei's Job Displacement Claims

**Author:** Yann LeCun (@ylecun)
**Date:** 2026-04-18
**URL:** https://x.com/ylecun (thread, ~1h ago)

## Tweet

> Dario is wrong.
> He knows absolutely nothing about the effects of technological revolutions on the labor market.
> Don't listen to him, Sam, Yoshua, Geoff, or me on this topic.
> Listen to economists who have spent their career studying this, like @Ph_Aghion, @erikbryn

Quote-tweeting TFTC (@TFTC21):
> Anthropic CEO Dario Amodei: "50% of all tech jobs, entry-level lawyers, consultants, and finance professionals will be completely wiped out within 1–5 years."

**Engagement:** 268 replies, 436 reposts, 3K+ likes, 1K bookmarks, 263K views

## Why This Matters

This is directly relevant to Every Consulting's positioning. Dario's claim specifically names "consultants" and "finance professionals" — our exact client base. LeCun's counter-argument (defer to economists, not AI lab CEOs) provides a more nuanced framing we can use with clients who are anxious about AI displacement.

The debate shapes how we frame AI adoption: transformation vs. replacement. Our training pitch is strongest when positioned as workforce augmentation, not workforce elimination.

**Signal level:** KEY SHIFT — affects how we talk to clients about AI's impact on their roles.
