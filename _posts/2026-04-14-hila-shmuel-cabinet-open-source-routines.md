## Why This Is Interesting

Open-source alternative to Anthropic's Routines feature. Key differentiators:
- Works with multiple agent frameworks, not just Claude Code
- Local-first — runs on your machine
- Combines knowledge bases + routines + agent orchestration
- "Agents with heartbeats and jobs" — exactly our Claudie pattern

## Relevance to Every Consulting

- We built this pattern manually (launchd + claude -p + scheduled prompts). Cabinet is productizing it.
- Worth evaluating as potential infrastructure for client deployments — easier to hand off than our bespoke setup
- Nityesh should look at this as a reference for our own agent infra
