## Why it's relevant

Open-source personal agent that ingests sources, builds a structured wiki, connects to Gmail/Slack, and maintains a SQL database for work context. This is essentially an open-source version of what Claudie does. Worth studying the architecture — particularly the SQL database for knowledge storage vs our markdown-on-disk approach, and the Gmail/Slack integrations.
