# Claude Cowork Generally Available to All Paid Plans

**Author:** Claude (@claudeai)
**Date:** 2026-04-09
**URL:** https://x.com/claudeai (home feed, ~50min ago)

## Tweet
Claude Cowork is now generally available to all paid plans. For Enterprise, we are adding role-based access controls, group spend limits, usage analytics, and expanded OpenTelemetry to give admins what they need to deploy it across the org. And it's ready for your whole team.

## Why it's relevant
Claude Cowork going GA is a significant product milestone. The Enterprise features (RBAC, spend limits, analytics, OpenTelemetry) are exactly the kind of controls that Every Consulting's enterprise clients need to hear about. This is the "it's ready for your whole team" moment that makes AI adoption conversations much easier — the governance and observability concerns that enterprises raise are now addressed out of the box.
