## Tweet

## Why This Is Interesting

Clean framing of the strategic pressure SaaS companies face:
- **From above**: Model companies (Anthropic, OpenAI, Google) are building end-user products that compete with existing SaaS
- **From below**: Startups building AI-native alternatives that are faster and cheaper

This is "historically challenging" — dual front wars rarely end well for the incumbent.
