# Levie's Law of AI Psychosis

**Author:** BuccoCapital Bloke (@buccocapital) quoting Aaron Levie
**URL:** https://x.com/buccocapital/status/2058614796530909536

> The farther away you are from the actual work the more confident you are that humans are no longer needed. CEOs are uniquely prone to AI psychosis because they're sufficiently distant from the last mile of work that still has to happen.

## Relevance
- **Natalia:** Name this dynamic in discovery — "executive and operator views of AI usually disagree; we work with both."
- **Mike/Brooker:** If a training isn't landing, check if the CEO scoped it but the users have different problems.
- **Curriculum:** "Last mile" framing is a clean teaching concept.
