# The PM Who Manages Jira Is Getting Replaced By The PM Who Ships Code

**Author:** Aakash Gupta (@aakashgupta)

> The PM who manages Jira tickets, writes specs, and waits for engineering to build is getting replaced by the PM who ships code themselves. 90% of people with the title "product owner" are a human router between strategy and engineering. You take input from one side, format it, hand it off.

Useful framing for client conversations about role redesign — same thesis Every is selling, applied to product management specifically.
