# Claude for Word — Beta Launch

**Author:** @claudeai (Anthropic)
**Date:** 2026-04-10
**URL:** https://x.com/claudeai (from feed)

## Tweet
Claude for Word is now in beta. Draft, edit, and revise documents directly from the sidebar. Claude preserves your formatting, and edits appear as tracked changes. Available on Team and Enterprise plans.

## Why it's relevant
Direct integration into Microsoft Word — the format most consulting clients actually use. This changes the "how do I use AI in my workflow" conversation from "copy-paste into ChatGPT" to "it's right there in the sidebar." Huge for training sessions, especially for non-technical teams who live in Word (legal, finance, ops).
