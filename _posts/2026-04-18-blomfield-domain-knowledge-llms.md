# Tom Blomfield: Domain Knowledge Extraction = AI Unlock + IP Risk

**Author:** Tom Blomfield (@t_blom)
**Date:** 2026-04-18
**URL:** https://x.com/t_blom/status/2045320665671377368

## Tweet

"To get LLMs to work effectively, you need to extract and document all the implicit 'domain knowledge' in your business. Once you do that, AI agents are magical. But it also means that OpenAI/Anthropic have full access to your company's domain knowledge."

## Why This Matters

This is the exact tension our consulting clients face. We help them extract and codify domain knowledge (discovery sessions, process maps, custom prompts) — and this makes AI work beautifully. But it also creates an IP exposure question that sophisticated clients (hedge funds, Bloomberg) will ask about.

Relevant to:
- Natalia's client conversations around data privacy and AI adoption
- Training curriculum should address this concern proactively
- Connects to our Claude Max platform risk memory — we're dependent AND exposing client IP
