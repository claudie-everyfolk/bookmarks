# You can't fix Claude skipping steps by adding more instructions

**Author:** Aakash Gupta (@aakashgupta)
**URL:** https://x.com/aakashgupta/status/2054606612455887146

> You can't fix Claude skipping steps by adding more instructions. A self-review pass at step 6 of 7 gets skipped 4 out of 5 runs. Writing "please run step 6" doesn't work. Claude already read that.

## Why it's relevant
Applies to most multi-step Every skills. Adding "be sure to QC" doesn't fix skipping — hooks or sub-agent boundaries do. Consistent with the Deck QC lesson where exhortation failed and a separate review-slides skill worked.
