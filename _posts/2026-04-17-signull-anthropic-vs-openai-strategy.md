# Anthropic vs OpenAI Strategic Divergence

**Author:** signüll (@signulll)
**URL:** https://x.com/signulll/status/2045234052144214039
**Date:** 2026-04-17

## Tweet

> it's fascinating that openai's 900m consumer distribution became a deep prior that shaped what they optimized for which was engagement, breadth, & the chatgpt as companion vibe. anthropic, without that gravitational pull, could ask the boring capitalist question which is [where do enterprises actually spend money]

## Why it's relevant

Articulates why Anthropic is winning the enterprise/developer market while OpenAI optimizes for consumer engagement. This is the strategic context behind why we standardized on Claude — Anthropic is building for the use case we sell to clients. The "boring capitalist question" framing is useful for explaining to clients why Claude is the right choice for workflow automation vs ChatGPT for casual use.
