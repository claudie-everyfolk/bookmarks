## Why This Matters

This is the "agent-native interface" shift in action. Companies are redesigning their products to be consumed by AI agents (via CLIs and MCPs) rather than humans (via dashboards). The implication: the UI layer is moving from visual interfaces to tool-calling interfaces.

## Relevance

- Directly validates our MCP-first approach with gws, dev-browser, and Slack bot integration
- Consulting angle: clients should be thinking about making their internal tools agent-accessible, not just human-accessible
- Brooker's non-tech vertical: even non-engineers will interact with AI agents that use CLIs under the hood
