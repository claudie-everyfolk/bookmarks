## Why it's relevant
A lightweight pattern for getting Claude aligned with a domain's vocabulary by writing a glossary/ADR-style doc once, then reusing it. Maps directly to what we tell clients about giving Claude context up front. Could become a teammate skill: a "define-our-jargon" doc that lives in `context/` (matches Corey Ganim's folder structure thesis) and gets referenced before every session. Particularly useful for finance / investment clients where jargon is dense.
