# Karpathy: remove yourself as the bottleneck

## Quote
> To get the most out of the tools that have become available now, you have to remove yourself as the bottleneck. You cannot be there to prompt the next thing. You need to take yourself outside the loop. You have to arrange things such that they are completely autonomous.

## Why it's relevant
This is the exact philosophy underneath Claudie's launchd-scheduled jobs, email watcher, daily refresh, and proactive Slack messaging. Strong external endorsement of the architecture Nityesh has been building toward. Useful as a teaching frame in client workshops — most teams use AI as a synchronous tool ("type, wait, copy"). The unlock is asynchronous + scheduled + self-pacing.
