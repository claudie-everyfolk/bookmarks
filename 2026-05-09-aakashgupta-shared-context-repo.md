# DoorDash PM uses shared repo as agent context

**Author:** Aakash Gupta (@aakashgupta)
**URL:** https://x.com/aakashgupta/status/2052966308225142901
**Date:** 2026-05-09

## Tweet

> Hannah Stulberg, a PM at DoorDash, built a shared repo where her team checks in every customer call summary, decision log, and analytics query. Last week a new engineer needed context on a customer decision from three months ago. Instead of pinging Hannah and waiting, the [agent answered from the repo].

## Why it's relevant

Mirrors Every Consulting's `~/work/` and `~/projects/` setup — client transcripts, decision logs, dashboards all checked in for Claudie to query. Concrete external validation of the pattern we already use, and a story Mike could repurpose in Tech-vertical training as social proof for clients hesitant about the "check everything in" discipline.
