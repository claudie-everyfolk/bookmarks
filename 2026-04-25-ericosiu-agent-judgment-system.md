# Your AI agents waste your time because they lack a system for judgment

Anecdote: agent shipped a blog post at 2am that hit every SEO benchmark — keywords, structure, internal links — but the take was wrong. Author killed it before it went live. Argues for an explicit judgment layer beyond mechanical rubrics.

## Why relevant
Maps to recurring problems in our pipeline (e.g., decks shipped without QC, fabricated stats in Thrive deck — both already in memory). Reinforces the value of the `review-slides` step and the "no fabricated claims" rule. Could be useful raw material for a training session on building review/judgment loops into client agents.
