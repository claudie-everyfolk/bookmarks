# Mollick: organizational design for agents is the next frontier

> Organizational design for agents is hard, benchmarking agents working in concert is hard. Together, this is the next critical frontier for making AI matter in economically valuable tasks.

Quoting rohit's essay "Aligned Agents Still Build Misaligned Organisations."

## Why relevant
Frames a problem Every Consulting clients are starting to hit: they have individual Claude skills working but no coherent multi-agent operating model. Useful framing for training decks and discovery conversations.
