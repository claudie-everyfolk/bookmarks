## Why It's Relevant

The "harness" framing is gaining traction. The question of one harness vs. many is exactly what our clients face: do they build one central AI system (like Claudie) or distribute AI capabilities across existing tools (Notion, Slack, etc.)? This is the architectural decision we help clients make in discovery sessions. Worth watching Notion's integration approach — their third approach from Anthropic is likely the tool-use/MCP pattern.
