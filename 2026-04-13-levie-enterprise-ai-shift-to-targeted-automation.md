# Enterprise AI: From Chatbots to Targeted Agentic Automation

**Author:** Brett Caughran (@FundamentEdge), citing Aaron Levie
**URL:** https://x.com/FundamentEdge/status/2043510887206838781
**Date:** 2026-04-13

## Tweet

The big shift in enterprise strategy: The bulk of internal AI work over the last 36 months has been about feeding context into multi-model chatbots, distributing tools to teams, building prompt libraries, and "let a thousand flowers bloom" approach. Nothing from this strategy has driven meaningful changes to institutional processes — just incremental use cases.

Growing realization that agentic workflows require a new enterprise strategy. As Aaron Levie says: "a shift to targeted automation efforts applied to specific areas of work and workflow" — which de-emphasizes the prior chatbot iteration.
