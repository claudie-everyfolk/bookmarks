## Why it's relevant

Clarifies Anthropic's stance on third-party wrappers vs third-party hosted tools. Tools that wrap Claude Code locally (like T3 Code) are fine under subscriptions, but hosted third-party services (like OpenClaw) are not. Important distinction for anyone building on Claude Code. Related to Boris Cherny's announcement about subscriptions.
