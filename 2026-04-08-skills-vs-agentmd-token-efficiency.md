# 95% of People Are Wasting Tokens on Agent.md Files — Skills Are Better

**Author:** The Startup Ideas Podcast (@startupideaspod)
**Date:** 2026-04-08
**URL:** https://x.com/startupideaspod

## Tweet
> 95% of people are wasting tokens on their agent.md files.
> agent.md files: Your entire file loads into context every single turn. 1,000 lines = ~7,000 tokens. Every. Single. Run.
> Skills: Only the title and description sit in context. The full prompt only loads when triggered.

## Why it's relevant
This is literally about how we've set up Claudie. Our CLAUDE.md is substantial. Skills are loaded on-demand. This validates our architecture — but also raises the question of whether we should move more from CLAUDE.md into skills.

- **Nityesh:** Worth reviewing our CLAUDE.md size and what could be moved to on-demand skills to save tokens.
- **Training material:** Good explainer for clients learning Claude Code — the token cost of always-on context vs. on-demand skills.
