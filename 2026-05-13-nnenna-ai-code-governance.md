> This is a major aspect of AI code governance that the team shipped today. An overview of PRs reviewed w/ stats + filtering across teams and repos. — how many issues were caught + fixed — which services carry the most risk — where review quality is degrading. The new findings...

## Why it's relevant
Most Every clients are starting to ask "how do we govern AI-written code?" once they get past pilot. This is a real, concrete answer pattern: roll AI reviewers into PRs and track the dashboard. Worth keeping as a reference architecture when clients ask the governance question, especially for engineering-heavy clients.

For internal use too — Mike has been thinking about how to measure delivery quality across engagements; the same pattern (rolling reviewer stats by team / service / risk surface) applies to consulting deliverables, not just code.
