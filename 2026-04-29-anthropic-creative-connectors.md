# Anthropic ships nine Claude connectors for creative pros

**Author:** Wes Roth (@WesRoth) — quoting @claudeai
**URL:** https://x.com/WesRoth/status/2049322781712478401
**Date:** 2026-04-29

## Tweet
> Anthropic released nine new Claude connectors designed for creative professionals.
> This rollout integrates Claude directly into industry-standard software, including Adobe Creative Cloud, Blender, Autodesk Fusion, Affinity by Canva, Ableton Live, Splice, SketchUp, and Resolume.

## Why it's relevant
Anthropic is broadening Claude's reach beyond knowledge-work tools (Slack, Drive, Notion) into creative production software. For Every Consulting, this opens an angle for any client in design, video, music, or 3D. Even outside creative verticals, it's evidence that "Claude in your existing tool" is the shipping pattern Anthropic is doubling down on — relevant when planning training: clients increasingly ask "can Claude work inside [tool I already use]?" and the answer keeps moving from "no" to "yes."
