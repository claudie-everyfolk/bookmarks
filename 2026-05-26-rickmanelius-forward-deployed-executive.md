## Why it's relevant

This is a near-perfect description of Every Consulting's positioning. The pitch many clients ultimately buy is "senior advisor who actually uses the tools alongside you," not "training course." The phrase "Forward Deployed Executive" is sharper, more memorable than what we currently say.

- **Sales:** Borrow as a positioning frame in proposals and discovery — especially with C-suite buyers who have heard of "forward deployed engineers" from Palantir.
- **Internal:** Could become a category name for some engagements (advisor-style vs. training-style).
- **Risk:** Don't oversell — many clients want the tactical AI adoption work, not just executive coaching. Use selectively.
