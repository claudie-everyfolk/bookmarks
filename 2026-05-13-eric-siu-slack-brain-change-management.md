# The chat-embedded brain is the actual AI change management win

**Author:** Eric Siu (@ericosiu)
**URL:** https://x.com/ericosiu/status/2054573137375924687
**Date:** 2026-05-13

> One of the best examples of actual AI change management succeeding is inserting a single brain into Slack/Teams that your company can work directly with. Their daily workflows already involve chat. Speaking with agents feels no different than working with humans.

## Why it's relevant
This is exactly what Claudie is for the Every team — a chat-embedded brain in Slack that the team works with as if it were a teammate. Validates the model. Useful for two things:
1. **Client work** — when clients ask "where do we start with AI?", the answer of "put a useful brain in your existing chat tool" beats "build a custom app." Lower friction, faster adoption.
2. **Sales / training narrative** — Natalia / Mike can point at Eric's framing when explaining why Every's engagements start with workflow-embedded AI rather than greenfield products.

Worth keeping in the back pocket as a third-party endorsement of Every's house pattern.
