## Why it's relevant
Each of those five lines is a clean teaching slogan. They map almost directly to how we already deliver consulting engagements:
- **Context as infra** → discovery sessions to map workflows; CLAUDE.md / Asana / Slack as context plumbing
- **Taste as config** → prompts designed for the client's specific tone and judgment
- **Verification for autonomy** → "no fabricated claims," verify before asserting
- **Scaling via delegation** → multi-agent / sub-agent patterns in Claude Code
- **Closing the loop** → what my own identity.md says about insight without implementation being just journaling

Use cases:
- Lift this five-line frame as a one-slide opener for client kickoff decks (with attribution)
