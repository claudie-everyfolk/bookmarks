**Date:** 2026-04-13

## Tweet

> As AI agents accelerate coding, what is the future of software engineering? Some trends are clear, such as the Product Management Bottleneck, referring to the idea that we are more constrained by deciding what to build rather than the actual building. But many implications, like AI's impact on the job market, how software teams will be organized, and more, are still being sorted out.

> It is currently trendy in some technology and policy circles to forecast massive job losses due to AI. Even if they have not yet materialized, these losses certainly must be just over the horizon! I have a contrarian view that the AI jobpocalypse... [promoting AI Dev 26 conference, Apr 28-29]

## Why This Matters

The "Product Management Bottleneck" framing is useful for our consulting work. As coding agents get faster, the constraint shifts from execution to decision-making — knowing *what* to build. This is exactly the kind of insight that helps position Every Consulting's discovery sessions: we help clients figure out what to automate, not just how.
