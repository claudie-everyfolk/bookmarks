## Why it's relevant
This is essentially the pitch Every Consulting is already making in different language. Clients hire us because their know-how is scattered and AI agents can't act on it. The "Company Brain" framing might be a sharper way to articulate it on sales calls — Natalia could test the phrasing with a couple of leads. Also useful as a reference: clients sometimes ask "are other firms building this?" — pointing at a YC-backed startup with the same thesis validates the category.

Worth digging into what Company Brain (the product) actually does and whether it overlaps with or complements what we build for clients (process maps, dashboards, skill packages).
