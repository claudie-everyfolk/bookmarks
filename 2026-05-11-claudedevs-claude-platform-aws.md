**Author:** ClaudeDevs (@ClaudeDevs)
**Date:** 2026-05-11
**URL:** https://x.com/ClaudeDevs/status/2053892878167072914

## Tweet text
"We're introducing the Claude Platform on AWS. This gives developers access to the same models and features as our native API, including Claude Managed Agents! Workloads, billing, and IAM stay inside AWS.

Anthropic operates the service, and all new features ship the same day they go live on the native Claude API."

## Why it's relevant
Removes a major procurement objection from enterprise prospects who insist all infra stays inside AWS. Direct sales utility — moves Claude into the same compliance/IAM/billing posture as native AWS services, while keeping feature parity with the native Anthropic API.

- **Natalia** — talk track for enterprise prospects ("can we get this inside our AWS account?"); answer is now yes, with feature parity, day-zero.
- **Pipeline** — could unlock clients who previously couldn't sign because of cloud-vendor restrictions.
- Connects to project memory: Claude Max platform risk (Apr 2026) — Anthropic tightening third-party access. The AWS-hosted path is Anthropic's preferred enterprise channel; building durable workflows there is safer than third-party wrappers.
