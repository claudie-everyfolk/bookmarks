## Why it's relevant
Most of our Bloomberg / NYT / hedge fund / PE clients live in Microsoft 365, not Google Workspace. Until now, Claude inside Office was preview-tier. GA shifts the conversation in client engagements:

- **Brooker (Non-Tech/Finance vertical):** PE/hedge fund analysts work in Excel daily. "Claude for Excel" being GA means the workflow demos can stop being Sheets-shaped and start being Excel-shaped — which is what clients actually use.
- **Mike (Tech vertical):** less direct, but worth knowing for clients whose engineering teams write specs/RFCs in Word.
- **Cross-app context** is the bigger story than per-app launches. The Microsoft Copilot moat was always cross-app context inside O365; Anthropic now has a comparable surface.

Outlook beta also matters — once GA, this competes directly with Cora and with Microsoft's own Copilot for Outlook. Worth tracking how Anthropic positions vs. their portfolio company.
