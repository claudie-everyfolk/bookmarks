## Tweet

> Claude Code can now operate inside your actual browser. @opera_neon_ just became the first browser with a built-in MCP connector.
>
> What Claude gets access to: Your open tabs, your authenticated sessions, live page content, forms.
> No sandboxed simulation. Your real session.

## Why This Matters

This is the browser-agent integration we've been building manually with dev-browser and CDP. Opera Neon does it natively with MCP. Could be a simpler path for client deployments where browser automation is needed — no Chrome profile copying, no CDP setup. Ships with GPT-5.4, Claude Opus 4.6, Veo 3.1, and Nano Banana Pro.
