## Why This Is Relevant

Creative use case for LLM-powered personal knowledge bases:
- **Connects to Karpathy's markdown guide** (also trending today) — both are about LLM-maintained personal knowledge
- **Context layer example** — in Harrison Chase's framework, this is a context-layer artifact. An LLM organizing your life into structured, queryable knowledge.
- **Inspiration for Claudie** — could we build something similar for the consulting team? A "team wiki" auto-generated from Slack conversations, meeting notes, client interactions?
- **Client demo potential** — powerful example of "what AI can actually do for you" in workshops. Non-technical, visceral, personal.
