## Why it's relevant

A blunt confirmation of the consulting wedge: most enterprises are still stuck. The MCP and multi-agent skepticism is interesting — both are technologies the team has bet some chips on. Worth tracking whether this is a contrarian opinion or a representative one.

- **For sales:** Useful counter-narrative against "we'll just buy a multi-agent platform." Positions Every's process-map / workflow-first approach as the alternative that actually moves needles.
- **For internal calibration:** Be cautious about over-investing in MCP-heavy solutions if the market sentiment turns. Watch for similar voices in coming weeks.
- **For training:** A good "where the market actually is" slide in executive onboarding sessions.
