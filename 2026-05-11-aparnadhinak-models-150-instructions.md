## Why relevant

Direct evidence to share with clients who are still hand-wringing about "the model won't follow my rules." A year ago the 150-200 instruction ceiling was real; now it's substantially higher. This is the kind of empirical update that should reshape how we coach clients to write long system prompts, CLAUDE.md files, and skill instructions.

Connects to ongoing client conversations:
- Training sessions where we tell clients to keep system prompts focused
- The recurring "should our CLAUDE.md be split into multiple files?" question (already bookmarked Pawel Huryn's tweet on the same topic today)
