## Why It's Relevant

This is the SaaS-to-agents shift in real time. Linear, PostHog, Attio all replacing dashboards with chat bars. The insight: it's not about chat UI, it's about domain-specific agents embedded in workflows. Directly relevant to our consulting work — clients using these tools will need to understand the agent layer, not just the chat interface.
