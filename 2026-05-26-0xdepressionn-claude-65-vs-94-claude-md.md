## Why it's relevant

The "same model, different output, the variable is CLAUDE.md" framing is gold for client training. Most clients still think the lever is model selection. This tweet reframes it as: the model is fixed, the instructions are the variable. That maps directly to what we teach.

- **For training:** Borrow the "65 vs 94" framing in workshop slides. Concrete numbers help justify why we spend session time writing CLAUDE.md content.
- **For sales:** Useful one-line answer to "should we wait for the next model?" — no, the model isn't your bottleneck.
- **Numbers caveat:** The 65/94 figures are illustrative, not benchmarked. Don't quote them as gospel; use them as a frame.
